# Use when a pitcher has both SP and RP Ottoneu eligibility but is currently
# used in only one role by their MLB team. Ottoneu only awards points when the
# pitcher is in the slot matching their actual MLB usage.
#

#   SP = currently in the rotation (won't fill RP slots)

#
